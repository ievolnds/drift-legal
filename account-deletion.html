刪除你的 drift 帳號 · Delete Your drift Account

最後更新 Last updated: 2026 年 7 月 14 日 (July 14, 2026)

本頁說明如何刪除你的 drift 帳號，以及刪除後會發生什麼事。

This page explains how to delete your drift account and what happens when you do.

在應用程式內刪除帳號Delete Your Account In the App

你可以隨時在 drift 應用程式內永久刪除你的帳號：

You can permanently delete your account anytime directly within the drift app:

  1. 開啟 drift，前往底部導覽列的「你」（you）頁面 Open drift and go to the "you" tab in the bottom navigation
  2. 點選右上角的齒輪圖示，進入「設定」 Tap the gear icon in the top right to open Settings
  3. 捲動至頁面底部，點選「刪除帳號」 Scroll to the bottom of the page and tap "刪除帳號" (Delete Account)
  4. 在確認對話框中再次確認，即完成永久刪除 Confirm in the dialog that appears — your account will be permanently deleted

刪除後會發生什麼事What Happens When You Delete Your Account

一旦確認刪除，你的帳號會立即且永久移除，此動作無法復原。以下資料會被永久刪除：

Once confirmed, your account is immediately and permanently removed. This action cannot be undone. The following data is permanently deleted:

  • 你的帳號與登入資訊 Your account and login credentials
  • 個人檔案（顯示名稱、自介、頭貼、年齡、性別、性向等） Your profile (display name, bio, avatar, age, gender, orientation, etc.)
  • 你發布過的所有貼文與照片 All posts and photos you've shared
  • 你參與過的對話與訊息紀錄 Conversations and messages you've participated in
  • 你的共鳴（resonates）、封鎖清單與其他帳號相關資料 Your resonates, block list, and other account-related data

「Now」等限時狀態本就是暫時性內容，會在發布一小時後自動且永久刪除，與帳號刪除無關。

Ephemeral content such as "Now" statuses is already automatically and permanently deleted one hour after posting, independent of account deletion.

刪除時程Deletion Timing

你的帳號與上述個人資料會在你確認刪除後立即從我們的系統中永久移除，我們不會為此保留備份副本。

Your account and the personal data listed above are immediately and permanently removed from our systems upon confirmation. We do not retain backup copies of this data.
