如何刪除我的帳號？How do I delete my account?

你可以隨時在應用程式內的「設定」頁面永久刪除帳號：前往底部導覽列的「你」→ 右上角齒輪圖示 → 捲動至頁面底部的「刪除帳號」。完整步驟與刪除後會發生什麼事，請見我們的刪除帳號說明頁。

You can permanently delete your account anytime from in-app Settings: the "you" tab → the gear icon top right → "刪除帳號" (Delete Account) at the bottom of the page. For the full steps and what happens after deletion, see our Account Deletion page.

忘記密碼怎麼辦？I forgot my password — what do I do?

drift 目前尚未提供應用程式內的自助密碼重設功能。請寄信至下方的 support 信箱，並附上你註冊時使用的 email，我們會協助你重新設定。

drift doesn't yet offer a self-service password reset inside the app. Please email us at the support address below with the email address you registered with, and we'll help you reset it.

如何檢舉不當內容？How do I report inappropriate content?

在貼文詳情頁，點選右上角的「⋯」選單 → 「檢舉這篇貼文」；在對話中，點選聊天室右上角的「⋯」選單 → 「檢舉這個使用者」。同一則內容被 3 位不同使用者檢舉後，會自動隱藏並交由我們審核。

On a post's detail page, tap the "⋯" menu in the top right → "檢舉這篇貼文" (Report this post). In a conversation, tap the "⋯" menu in the chat header → "檢舉這個使用者" (Report this user). Once the same content is reported by 3 different users, it's automatically hidden pending our review.

為什麼 drift 需要定位權限？Why does drift need location permission?

drift 是以城市為單位的社群應用程式，定位權限用來判斷你所在的城市、顯示附近的內容，以及計算你與其他內容的概略距離。我們絕不會將你的精確座標透露給其他使用者——其他人只會看到模糊化後的概略距離或城市層級資訊。你可以隨時在裝置的系統設定中關閉此權限，但部分依賴位置的功能將無法使用。詳見我們的隱私權政策第 3 節。

drift is a city-based social app; location permission is used to determine your city, show nearby content, and calculate approximate distances to other content. We never reveal your precise coordinates to other users — others only see an obfuscated approximate distance or city-level information. You can revoke this permission anytime in your device settings, though some location-dependent features won't work. See Section 3 of our Privacy Policy for details.
