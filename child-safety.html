兒童安全標準 · Child Safety Standards

最後更新 Last updated: 2026 年 7 月 15 日 (July 15, 2026)

drift 致力於為所有使用者打造一個安全的環境，並對兒童性虐待與剝削內容（CSAE）採取零容忍態度。本頁說明我們的相關政策與具體作法，符合 Google Play「兒童安全標準」揭露要求。

drift is committed to providing a safe environment for all users and takes a zero-tolerance stance on child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) content. This page describes our related policies and practices, in line with Google Play's Child Safety Standards disclosure requirements.

1. 零容忍政策1. Zero Tolerance Policy

drift 嚴格禁止任何形式的兒童性虐待與剝削內容（Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation, CSAE）出現在平台上、被分享，或以任何方式被促成。這項規範沒有例外。

drift strictly prohibits any form of child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) content from appearing on, being shared through, or otherwise being facilitated by the platform. There are no exceptions to this rule.

一旦發現違反本政策的帳號或內容，我們將立即移除該內容並永久停權相關帳號，且依法向主管機關通報，包括但不限於美國國家失蹤與受剝削兒童中心（NCMEC）以及台灣與使用者所在地之適用執法機關。

Upon discovering an account or content that violates this policy, we immediately remove the content and permanently ban the associated account, and report it to the relevant authorities as required by law, including but not limited to the U.S. National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) and applicable law enforcement agencies in Taiwan and the user's jurisdiction.

2. 年齡限制2. Age Requirement

drift 僅供年滿 18 歲的使用者使用。此規範明確載明於使用者授權合約（End User License Agreement, EULA）中，使用者於應用程式內的註冊流程（onboarding）必須完整閱讀並主動同意該合約，才能繼續使用 drift。

drift is intended only for users aged 18 and older. This requirement is explicitly stated in our End User License Agreement (EULA), which users must read in full and actively accept during the in-app onboarding flow before they can continue using drift.

此外，使用者於註冊時須提供年齡資訊，系統會在後端驗證並拒絕未滿 18 歲者完成註冊。

In addition, users are required to provide their age at sign-up, and our backend validates this and rejects registration for anyone under 18.

3. 檢舉機制3. Reporting Mechanism

任何使用者都可以在應用程式內對貼文或使用者提出檢舉：在貼文詳情頁點選右上角的「⋯」選單選擇「檢舉這篇貼文」，或在聊天室點選右上角的「⋯」選單選擇「檢舉這個使用者」，並選擇檢舉原因送出。

Any user can report a post or another user directly within the app: on a post's detail page, tap the "⋯" menu in the top right and select "檢舉這篇貼文" (Report this post); in a conversation, tap the "⋯" menu in the chat header and select "檢舉這個使用者" (Report this user), then select a reason and submit.

同一則內容一旦累積來自 3 位不同使用者的檢舉，系統會自動將其隱藏，不再對一般使用者顯示，並進入管理員審核佇列等待人工複核。涉及兒童安全的檢舉會被優先處理（見第 5 節）。

Once the same content accumulates reports from 3 different users, the system automatically hides it from general users and places it in an admin review queue for manual follow-up. Reports involving child safety are prioritized (see Section 5).

4. 內容審核4. Content Moderation

除了使用者檢舉，我們也提供多層機制維護平台安全：

Beyond user reports, we maintain several layers of safety mechanisms:

  • 自動隱藏：累積 3 人檢舉的貼文自動隱藏（見上）。
    Auto-hide: content reported by 3 different users is automatically hidden (see above).
  • 封鎖：使用者可隨時封鎖他人，封鎖後雙方將互相看不到彼此的內容、無法互相聯繫。
    Blocking: users can block anyone at any time; once blocked, neither party can see the other's content or contact each other.
  • 帳號停權：管理員可依審核結果永久停用違規帳號，停權帳號無法再使用 drift 任何功能。
    Account suspension: admins can permanently suspend accounts found to be in violation, after which the account can no longer use any drift feature.
  • 管理員審核工具：我們有內部管理後台，可檢視完整檢舉清單（含被檢舉內容、檢舉人數、目標帳號）、隱藏/恢復貼文、停用帳號、將不成立的檢舉標記結案。所有審核操作皆需二次確認並留下稽核紀錄。
    Admin review tools: our internal admin dashboard lets reviewers see the full report queue (reported content, report counts, target accounts), hide/restore posts, suspend accounts, and dismiss unfounded reports. Every moderation action requires confirmation and is logged for audit.

5. 聯絡方式5. Contact

若你發現、懷疑或想回報任何與兒童安全相關的內容或行為（包括 CSAE），請立即透過以下信箱與我們聯繫。我們會將兒童安全相關的檢舉列為最優先處理事項。

If you discover, suspect, or want to report any content or behavior related to child safety (including CSAE), please contact us immediately at the email below. We treat child safety reports as our highest priority.

Email: support@drift-date.com

6. 法規遵循6. Legal Compliance

我們遵循台灣與其他適用法域關於兒童保護的相關法律規範。針對涉及兒童安全的執法機關與主管機關合法調查請求，我們將依法配合，包括在法律要求的範圍內保存、提供必要的帳號與內容資訊，以協助調查與追訴。

We comply with applicable child protection laws in Taiwan and other relevant jurisdictions. For lawful investigation requests from law enforcement and regulatory authorities concerning child safety, we cooperate as required by law, including preserving and providing necessary account and content information within the scope required by law to assist investigation and prosecution.
